skill 5

  • Create a script that demonstates the rule of 72 to the user.
    The rule of 72 indicates the approximate number of years it will take an investement to double. For example, for a 9% rate of return, 72 / 9 , it would take 8 years for the investement to double.
  • The user can enter an initial investment in a textbox and an interest rate in another textbox.
  • Write a script that takes these values and returns with a brief statement in the answerbox. If the user entered $5000 and 9%, the statement would read: "You will have $10000 in about 8 years".
  • Note: include a $ with the amount, but do NOT worry about commas.

Use toString(1) to limit the years decimal to 1 place.

This skill is worth 14 points
